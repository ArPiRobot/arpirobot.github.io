def arpirobot.arduino.sensor.NxpAdafruit9Dof.calibrate (   self,
int  samples 
)

Calibrate the IMU.

Should reduce gyro drift and accelerometer error IMU MUST BE STATIONARY DURING CALIBRATION. GRAVITATIONAL ACCELERATION MUST BE IN NEGATIVE Z DIRECTION DURING CALIBRATION.

Parameters
samplesThe number of samples to take during calibration. More samples may be more accurate, but will take longer.
